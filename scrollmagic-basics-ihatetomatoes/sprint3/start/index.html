The Maintenance Supervisor

A short story

The Problem

Meet Duncan, Age 45, works with MaintOne

He has issues trusting the 'system'
He logs into MaintOne
He then uses the notification numbers to go into 1SAP to look at the maintenance history
He is also using the specific IP address of the truck to go into the actual details of the truck
And if relevant, he might also go into tyre sense to look at the tyre info
He then, based on his experience, figures out what all the information means in his head and makes a judgement call on whether it’s just an issue with sensors or it’s problem with the truck which needs to be resolved.

The Problem

The User Story

As a Maintenance Supervisor, I want to have the system report to me on the critical events across the fleet of trucks so that I have the required data to make informed decisions about what work orders to carry out for the purpose or reducing tyre wear.
